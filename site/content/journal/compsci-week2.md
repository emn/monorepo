after a second week of study with MathAcademy i am less enthusiastic about it.
the gamification has only discouraged me from trying too hard, because you get no 'XP' when you fail a question, regardless of how much time you spent on it.
i knew that the content was 'aligned' with school curricula, but i feel that it has also been designed to get students to be able to pass exams as quickly as possible, without regard to a deeper understanding or intution about the mathematics.
i already find myself able to answer questions by performing certain mechanical steps, while having no idea what i am really doing or why.
it's a shame because they are definitely on the right track with their utilization of spaced repetition.
i will continue for now, but i may not pay for a second month of access and instead search for an alternative (which will hopefully be cheaper).
